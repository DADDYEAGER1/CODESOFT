### Steps

1. Go to: [Create New Item](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Special:NewItem)

3. Click **"Create"**

After creation, add these properties by clicking **"+ add statement"**:

| **country (P17)** | `United States` (Q30) | Search your country |
